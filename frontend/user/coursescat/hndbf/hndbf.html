Higher National Diploma in Business Finance
(HNDBF)

Course Duration

This Higher National Diploma in Business Finance provides students with a strong foundation education in Business and Finance. Students will also be provided with specialized knowledge in selected areas of study with accompanied technical skills in finance, management, and business strategy. In the process the programme will enable the students to develop analytical, logical, critical and problem-solving skills and personal attributes such as communication and presentation skills
