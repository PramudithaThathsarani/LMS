Course Duration

The course duration consisted of five semesters namely Semester I, Semester II, Semester III, Semester IV and Semester V, covered over a period of two and half calendar years. Academic activities were held during the first four semesters and during the fifth semester students were required to complete a full‚Äêtime industrial placement.
